From KTrivia to KBattle
The screenshot on the left is how the first iteration of K-Battle looked like.
The screenshot on the right is how it looks like now.
Prevouis Designs
Originially KBattle was a game where you play against another player spin the wheel and based on the subject you land on thats the type of question you would be asked.
After lettings users test the game they tended to only like one type of question which was the "Guess the song" questions where they listened to a snippet of a song and had to guess which song it was.

Current Designs
I took that feeback and completely redesign the app. Now the game revolves around the idea of having to guess the Kpop song.
I also updated it so now you can play wiht 5 other players and you can see who is currently in the lead at the top of the screen.
This overall made the users gaming experience more fun and exciting.
